Oakland Maritime Support Services

Services for Truckers & the Port Community

OMSS is a green state-of-the art support service facility that acts as a physical hub for trucking activity and the other activities at the port of Oakland. We support truckers and the larger community at the port and in transit through the port.

Reducing pollution in West oakland

OMSS is fulfilling the West Oakland Community Action Plan to decrease pollution from diesel trucks by centralizing truck parking and services in one location and thus reducing truck idling and trips.

The Future -- The Oakland HUB
OMSS is transitioning to become the OAKLAND HUB and to develop a multi-faceted 16-acre transportation services facility  property that offers the right mix of parking and services to create a services destination for trucks. The Oakland Hub will includes auto & truck fueling stations (with biodiesel), a convenience market, a restaurant food court, office space, parking, container storage, truck scales, a trans-load facility.
‍
The Hub that will serve as a communal hub for pedestrians/bicyclists from Bay Bridge path AND as a complete-service center for truckers and community of the Port of Oakland (including: scales, truck fueling and auto-fuel, tire sales, truck repairs, trans-loading services, truck wash, medical services, convenience retail & foods court, insurance & DMV services, trucking office space for rent).
